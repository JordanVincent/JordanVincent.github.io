Mission Statement:

Because Bridge Disability Ministries honors the God-given dignity and giftedness of people with disabilities, we build relationships to alleviate isolation and enrich our churches and community.

Available Services

We offer more than just Guardianship

 

 

Standby

Bridge offers peace of mind to families who want more than a signed legal document on file. Bridge wants to know the person served and keep up with developments in the person's life.

As Standby, Bridge Ministries does the following:

--Make annual in-person visits with the person to be served

--Review the family Guardian's Reports to the court, and

--Review the annual Assessments of the Developmental Disabilities Administration (DDA) and other documents the family wants to provide.

 

Case Management

For an hourly rate, we can help with whatever you need, including:

--Housing selection/relocation

--DSHS - DDD assessments

--Health issue advocacy

--Social and Faith connections
