Available Items

If you see we have a GOOD SUPPLY of the items you need, come to our center during open hours. (No need to call or email.)

 

MOBILITY EQUIPMENT**

We usually have a Good Supply of:

--Canes: single point & quad

--Crutches: Standard & forearm

--Walkers: front wheel; walkers with forearm platforms

 

We have a Fluctuating Supply of:

--Bariatric Equipment

--Pedriatric Equipment

--Walkers: knee walkers; 3 wheel walkers; hemi walkers; 4 wheel walkers with seat;

--Wheel Chairs: rigid seat/back; standard folding; standard transport (16-18"); Tilt n Space

 

BATHING & TOILET EQUIPMENT**

We usually have a Good Supply of:

--Commodes - standard

--Toilet risers - regular

 

We have a Fluctuating Supply of:

--Transfer benches

--Commodes - drop arm & bariatric

--Shower chairs & stools

--Toilet risers with arms; Toilet rails

 

We have Very Few or Out of Stock:

shower chairs on wheels; Commodes on wheels; wall mount grab bars

 

MOVEMENT, FALL PREVENTION & MISCELLANEOUS**

We usually have a Good Supply of: Hoyer lifts; gait belts; floor peddles; tub grab bars

We have a Fluctuating Supply of: Grabbers; sock aids; transfer boards; bed rails.

 

HOSPITAL BEDS & LIFT CHAIRS **

We have a Good Supply of: Semi Electric Beds (suggested donation $250-$300)

We have a Limited Supply of: Fully Electric Beds (suggested donation $400-$500)

We have Very Few or Out of Stock: Bariatric Fully Electric Beds (suggested donation $700-$800)

Lift chairs: please call or email.

 

POWER EQUIPMENT

For info on power wheel chairs and power scooters, click here.

 

If you see we have a GOOD SUPPLY of the items you need, come to our center during open hours. (No need to call or email.)

 

For all other items, please call 425-885-1008 or email to confirm availability.

**If you call or email to ask about equipment it always helps us to know the height and weight of the person using the equipment. AND, please include your phone # in emails.

 

Open Hours: 11 am - 5:00 pm, Tuesday - Thursday
